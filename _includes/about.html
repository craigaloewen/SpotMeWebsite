About

Spot Me is an Open Source application that uses a Microsoft Kinect to determine the position of your skeleton, analyze it, and then give you personalized advice on the way to improve your form when doing an exercise. If you’re new to weights it will coach you on how to do the exercise properly, and if you’re experienced it will show you where your form can be improved. Spot Me is made in mind for people who want to critique their form in their living room, and especially useful for any handicapped individual, as many gyms are not handicapped accessible. Spot Me is provided under the Apache License 2.0.
